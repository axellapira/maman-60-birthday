A very Happy 60th Birthday to Anna Birch from her Ricciuti family in the United States.

Uncle Paul · cousins Bruce Ricciuti and Kara Ricciuti Manchester · her late Aunt Katherine Birch Ricciuti · cousin Katherine Ricciuti Kline

The Ricciuti family is connected to the Birch family when Anna's father, James Anthony "Tony" Birch, met Paul James Ricciuti in college. Tony introduced Paul to his sister, Katie Birch — and they married in 1963, joining the two families.

The Birch & Ricciuti children spent time in the late 1960s and early 1970s at Grandma Birch's home in Youngstown, Ohio. The family reunited in the 2000s — Bruce and Anna spent time together with family in Boston, and many emails followed with cousins.

Sebastian and family attended Paul's 90th birthday in 2025. And in September 2025, Anne and Paul met in London for High Tea, and Bruce and Anna also met in London.
